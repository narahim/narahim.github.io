Md Noor-A-Rahim

Assistant Professor | Lecturer
School of Computer Science & IT
University College Cork

Md. Noor-A-Rahim is currently serving as an Assistant Professor (Lecturer-Above the Bar) within the School of Computer Science and Information Technology at University College Cork, Ireland. Prior to this role, he was a Senior Researcher and Marie Curie Fellow within the same school. He also held the position of Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Dr. Rahim earned his Ph.D. degree from the School of Information Technology and Mathematical Sciences, University of South Australia, Australia, in 2015. In recognition of his academic excellence, he was honored with the Michael Miller Medal for presenting the most outstanding Ph.D. thesis in 2015.
