## Open Positions: SHIELD Project (2 PhDs, 1 Postdoc)

We are currently hiring **2 fully-funded PhD students** and **1 Postdoctoral Researcher** for the **SHIELD** project, funded as part of the **Frontiers for the Future Programme** from **Research Ireland**.

<ul style="list-style-type: none; padding-left: 0;">
  <li>🎓 <strong>PhD Positions:</strong> 48 months, fully-funded (Tax-free stipend of €25,000 p.a.)</li>
  <li>🔬 <strong>Postdoc Position:</strong> 24 Months, Fixed-Term, Whole-time Post. Salary: €46,306 - €52,863 p.a. (possibility of extension up to 48 months, contingent on the candidate’s performance and the continued availability of funding)</li>
  <li>🗓️ <strong>Start Date:</strong> March 2026</li>
  <li>⏰ <strong>Application Deadline:</strong> 7th January 2026</li>
</ul>

For more details on the project, please visit the <a href="{{ '/projects/SHIELD/' | relative_url }}">SHIELD project page</a>.

### Requirements

We are looking for candidates with the following qualifications:

<ul style="list-style-type: none; padding-left: 0;">
  <li>📚 <strong>Background:</strong> We are seeking applicants with experience and interest in one or more of the following areas: wireless networks and communications, machine learning and computer vision, intelligent transportation systems, intelligent sensing and localization, and signal processing.</li>
  <li>📄 <strong>Publication Record:</strong> Strong publication record with previous publications in reputed venues.</li>
  <li>💻 <strong>Programming:</strong> Strong programming competency is a must.</li>
  <li>🗣️ <strong>Language:</strong> Must meet the English language requirements provided by University College Cork (UCC).</li>
</ul>

**Specific Requirements:**
<ul style="list-style-type: none; padding-left: 0;">
  <li>🎓 <strong>PhD Candidates:</strong> Must hold a Master's degree.
    <ul style="list-style-type: none; padding-left: 20px;">
      <li><strong>Note:</strong> Successful non-EU candidates may need to apply for a non-EU tuition fee waiver to get full tuition fee waivers.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>🔬 <strong>Postdoc Candidates:</strong> A candidate with a recent PhD will be preferred (expertise in Wireless Networks and AI/ML is essential). Candidates who will be finishing their PhD soon are also strongly encouraged to apply, provided they have a strong publication record.</li>
</ul>
