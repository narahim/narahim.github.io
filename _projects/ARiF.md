<div style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.8;">
    <p>Digital transformation is significantly impacting the forest sector, with the proliferation of digital data enhancing a range of applications from remote sensing to Industry 4.0. A lot of data is currently being collected and assessed in the forest sector. However, there is a widespread lack of applications to use this digital data and a lack of coordinated, interdisciplinary research.</p>

    <p>Augmented Reality (AR), a futuristic technology that integrates digital elements into the real world, has become essential for visualising and interacting with these data. AR is a promising solution for transforming forestry practices, from fieldwork to the timber trade. Challenges include unreliable network coverage in remote forests, the need for robust and durable devices and solutions, and inaccurate positioning under the forest canopy.</p>
